Intro

In the middle of June, my girlfriend and I went to Madison together. She had never been, so it was my opportunity to show her the city and introduce her to some of my friends. It was a highlight of my summer to be back in my favorite city!

Friday

The first full day we were there, we spent the day shopping up and down state street.

After shopping, We went to Memorial Union for some ice cream on the terrace, a classic Madison experience! Then we went up Observatory Hill to get a good view of Lake Mendota.

Saturday

The next day, we went to the Dane County Farmers' Market. It was miserably hot so we did not stay for long, however we did make it up onto the observatory deck of the Capitol building.

We spent the rest of the day visiting with my friends. We joined some friends at Vilas Beach, then hung out with another group of friends at a their house. It was great to see them!

Sunday

We left Madison around 10 a.m. On the way back to Minnesota, we stopped at The House on the Rock. It was incredible and I would highly reccomend it to anyone who has the chance to go.
